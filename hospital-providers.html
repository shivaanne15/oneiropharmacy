Parenteral Nutrition

Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN)

Customized parenteral nutrition, compounded to order for your patients.

Oneiro Pharmacy compounds patient-specific TPN formulations in our USP <797> compliant cleanrooms. Every bag is prepared to your prescriber's exact specifications and verified by our pharmacists before release, so your facility receives safe, accurate parenteral nutrition on a dependable schedule.

  • Fully customized adult, pediatric, and neonatal TPN formulations
  • 2-in-1 and 3-in-1 (total nutrient admixture) preparations
  • Electrolyte, vitamin, and trace element adjustments per patient labs
  • Daily and cyclic delivery schedules to match your census
  • STAT compounding for urgent new starts and formula changes
  • 24/7 pharmacist consultation for your clinical team

IV Antibiotics

Ready-to-administer intravenous antibiotic therapy for your facility.

Our sterile compounding team prepares intravenous antibiotic doses for hospitals, surgical centers, and acute care facilities. Preparations are compounded in compliant cleanrooms with strict beyond-use dating, labeling, and quality controls — reducing the burden on your in-house pharmacy while maintaining continuity of therapy.

  • Broad range of IV antibiotic and antimicrobial preparations
  • Patient-specific and batch-prepared doses (IV bags, syringes, elastomeric pumps)
  • Extended-infusion and continuous-infusion preparations
  • Strict USP <797> sterility, labeling, and beyond-use dating standards
  • STAT turnaround for urgent starts and dose changes
  • Reliable scheduled deliveries, 365 days a year
